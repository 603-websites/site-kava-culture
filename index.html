BRINGING KAVA TO THE PEOPLE

Born in Southwest Florida, Kava Culture Kava Bar has brought a refreshing friendly atmosphere where you can hangout and grab an ice cold drink with zero alcohol. Enjoy a traditional Kava Shell from the Kava root found in the exotic and earthy South Pacific. This beverage is still made in a traditional way, squeezing the root and drinking from coconut shells. A natural muscle relaxer, kava gives you a sense of calm and wellbeing without affecting cognitive or motor skills.
