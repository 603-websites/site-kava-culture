Nashua's Alcohol-Free Kava Bar & Live Music Venue

The Spot is downtown Nashua's kava bar and live music venue at 217 Main Street. We serve premium kava, craft coffee from Anchorhead Coffee, creative mocktails, and food from Joanne's Kitchen—all in an alcohol-free, all-ages social lounge.

Open Monday through Thursday 3–11PM and Friday–Saturday 3PM–1AM, The Spot hosts live music, open mic nights, DJ sets, trivia, karaoke, comedy, and community events every week. No cover charge. No alcohol. Just good vibes.
