THE GOOD VIBES

YOUR SPOT IN NASHUA

BRINGING KAVA TO NASHUA

Nestled in the heart of downtown Nashua, The Spot is your community hub for connection, culture, and kava. We serve premium kava sourced directly from Pacific Island suppliers, alongside craft coffee, creative mocktails, and tasty bites — all in a welcoming, alcohol-free environment. From intimate acoustic sets to high-energy local bands, our dedicated music corner keeps the vibes flowing every week.

BOTANICALS ON TAP

We pour a bountiful variety of sparkling botanical elixirs on tap for elevated happiness. Our bartenders will happily guide you on a journey to your better self. All of our taps are crafted with love to uplift your mood, spirit, and deliver a vibrant sense of wellbeing. Pick your vibe!
