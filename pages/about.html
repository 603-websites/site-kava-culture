About Us
Family Run, Established in 2017

Born in the heart of Southwest Florida, Kava Culture was founded with one mission in mind: bring kava to the people.

OUR STORY

Established in 2017

Co-founders Jacqueline and Caroline Rusher wanted to build a space where communities could connect for work and play, where respecting people, plants, and the planet is a sacred practice. A space where the thousands of years of tribal kava culture and modern community spaces can meet, mix and mingle. And thus, Kava Culture was born.

After opening their first location in Bonita Springs, Florida, the mermaid sisters knew there was bigger potential to positively impact more communities. In less than 4 years, Jacqueline and Caroline have opened a total of seven corporate Kava Culture locations, using only earned capital from their initial investment to drive their passion-fueled expansion.

We're thrilled to invite you to the tribe, and honored to share our journey to DRINK LOVE with you. Shells up — BULA!
