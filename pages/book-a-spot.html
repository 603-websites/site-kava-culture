GOOD TO KNOW

WHAT TO EXPECT

Response Time

We review every submission within 48 hours. If your act is a good fit, we'll reach out to confirm the date and go over details.

The Venue

Intimate setting in the heart of downtown Nashua. Our dedicated music corner comfortably fits solo acts through full bands (up to 5-6 members).

Equipment Provided

We have a PA system, microphones, and basic stage lighting. Let us know your setup needs and we'll make it work.

What We Look For

All genres welcome. We love original music, local talent, and artists who bring positive energy. Covers are welcome too.

Scheduling

Live music nights are typically Friday and Saturday evenings. We also book weeknight acoustic sets and special events.

The Vibe

The Spot is alcohol-free, all-ages, and community-focused. Think chill kava bar meets listening room. All shows are free entry.

HAVE QUESTIONS?

Reach out to us directly on Instagram or through our contact page. We're always happy to chat about booking.
