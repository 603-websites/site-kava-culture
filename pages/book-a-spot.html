WHAT TO EXPECT

Response Time

We review every submission within 48 hours. If your act is a good fit, we'll reach out to confirm the date and go over details.

The Venue

Intimate setting in the heart of downtown Nashua. Our dedicated music corner comfortably fits solo acts through full bands (up to 5-6 members).

Equipment Provided

We have a PA system, microphones, and basic stage lighting. Let us know your setup needs and we'll make it work.

What We Look For

All genres welcome. We love original music, local talent, and artists who bring positive energy. Covers are welcome too.

Scheduling

Live music nights are typically Friday and Saturday evenings. We also book weeknight acoustic sets and special events.

The Vibe

The Spot is alcohol-free, all-ages, and community-focused. Think chill kava bar meets listening room. All shows are free entry.

VENUE & STAGE DETAILS

Performance Area

Dedicated performance corner with room for solo acts through full bands (up to 5-6 members). Intimate listening-room atmosphere — your audience is there for the music.

Sound & Lighting

Full PA system, microphones, and stage lighting provided. Just bring your instruments. Need something specific? Let us know in your request and we'll make it work.

Audience & Capacity

Seats 40-50 guests in an engaged, respectful environment. All shows are free entry, which keeps the room full. Alcohol-free venue means a focused, attentive crowd.

Genres Welcome

Acoustic, rock, jazz, blues, folk, singer-songwriter, hip-hop, electronic, spoken word, comedy, and open mic. Original music and covers both welcome.

Merch & Promotion

Sell your merch at every show — we'll set up a table for you. We promote every booked act on our Instagram, Facebook, and newsletter to our local audience.

Available Nights

Friday and Saturday evenings are prime slots (8 PM-11 PM). Weeknight acoustic sets and special events available on select days. Book 2-3 weeks ahead for best availability.

PERFORMER FAQ

How do I book a show at The Spot?
Fill out the booking request form above with your act name, genre, music samples, and preferred date. We review every submission within 48 hours and reach out to confirm if it's a good fit.
What equipment do you provide?
We provide a full PA system, microphones, basic stage lighting, and a dedicated performance area. Bring your instruments and we'll handle the sound. Let us know any special setup needs in your booking request.
How big is the stage?
Our dedicated performance area comfortably fits solo acts through full bands of 5-6 members. It's an intimate setting designed for close connection between performers and the audience.
What's the capacity?
The Spot seats around 40-50 guests in an engaged, respectful environment. All shows are free entry, which keeps the room full. Our alcohol-free setting means a focused, attentive crowd.
Do you pay performers?
Compensation varies by event. We work with performers on a case-by-case basis depending on the type of event, night, and expected turnout. Details are discussed once your booking request is approved.
Can I sell merch?
Yes! We encourage performers to bring and sell their own merchandise. We'll set aside space for your merch table near the performance area.
What nights are available?
Friday and Saturday evenings are prime slots (8 PM-11 PM). We also book weeknight acoustic sets and special events on select weekdays. Let us know your preferred date and we'll work with you.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date. Popular weekend slots fill up fast, so earlier is better. We accept bookings up to 3 months out.
