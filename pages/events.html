Live Music Corner

YOUR STAGE IS WAITING

The Spot is home to Nashua's most welcoming music corner. From singer-songwriters and acoustic duos to full bands and genre-bending performers, we celebrate local talent every week in a room that was built for community.

Sip a kava shell, grab a craft latte, and let the music take you somewhere new. All shows are alcohol-free, all-ages, and always free to enjoy.
