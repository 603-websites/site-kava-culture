NOT YOUR AVERAGE ALL NATURAL ELIXIR & KAVA BAR FRANCHISE

Our culture is centered around the local communities our bars exist with our encouragement of diversity, inclusivity and progressive innovation.

Sisters Jacqueline Rusher and Caroline Rusher founded Kava Culture Bar in 2017, bringing Kava to the people. With a diverse culture and all ages from college kids to grandparents enjoying the social atmosphere, the brand has taken off tremendously.

Now opening locations in multiple states and further developing and expanding with a strong upward trend. Where we say "Drink Love", our teas are genuinely brewed with passion and love.

We invite everyone with open arms and BULA together; to celebrate our lives, our paths crossing. If you're ready to invest in your future through franchising, learn more about this exciting opportunity!
