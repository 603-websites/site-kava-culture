NOT YOUR AVERAGE KAVA BAR & LIVE MUSIC FRANCHISE

The Spot is centered around the local communities our bars exist in, with our encouragement of diversity, inclusivity, and progressive innovation.

The Spot was born out of a passion for bringing people together in a relaxed, alcohol-free environment. With a diverse community and all ages from college kids to grandparents enjoying the social atmosphere and live music, the brand has taken off tremendously.

Now expanding and developing with a strong upward trend, The Spot combines premium kava, creative elixirs, and a vibrant live music scene into one unforgettable experience.

We invite everyone with open arms to celebrate our lives and our paths crossing. If you're ready to invest in your future through franchising, learn more about this exciting opportunity!
