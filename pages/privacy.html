PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: April 17, 2026

The Spot Kava Bar & Music ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website thespotnashua.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information you voluntarily provide or that is generated through your use of our website. This includes your email address when you subscribe to our newsletter, your name, email, and message when you submit our contact form, and details such as your band name, contact information, preferred dates, and genre when you submit a booking request. We also collect basic analytics data, including page views, referral sources, and session activity, to understand how visitors use our site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to inquiries submitted through our contact form, send occasional newsletters about upcoming events and specials to subscribers, process and review booking requests from artists and performers, and improve our website based on how visitors interact with it. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses a custom analytics system hosted at websiteupgraderpro.com to process page views and form submissions. This data is used solely for improving our website and is not shared with advertisers. Our contact page also includes a Google Maps embed to help visitors find our location, which is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as it is needed to fulfill the purposes described above. Newsletter subscriber emails are kept until you unsubscribe. Contact form messages and booking requests are retained as long as necessary to respond and follow up.

Your Rights

You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in every email. If you would like to request deletion of any personal data we hold about you, please contact us using the information below and we will process your request promptly.
